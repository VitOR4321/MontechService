Project on Marella Discovery 2

Jan/Feb 2022

During Marella Discovery II dry dock in Cadiz, Spain. Montech Serwis had a team onboard.

  • Prefabrication of 6 new cabanas in our workshop in Poland and installing them on the ship,
  • Prefabricating and installing 2 new stainless-steel showers in glasshouse,
  • Steel repair of corroded plate,
  • All stainless-steel work in remodeling of bars,
  • All water connection and new grey and black water lines for rebuilding new public toilets,
  • Installation of toilets, vanity units and urinals,
  • Sprinkler modification.
