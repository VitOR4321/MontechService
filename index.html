About Us :

The founders have long experience in the shipbuilding, ship repair and oil and gas sector. Over the years we have completed many successful projects with many of the biggest names in the cruise and oil and gas sector.

We are your full service marine contractor. From Prefabrication in our workshop in Gdansk to dry dock we will be there for you. We offer a wide range of services and special welding.
